FaQs

Can I modify a recipe to fit my dietary requirements?

Absolutely! You can swap out ingredients in order to make the meals more suitable to your dietary requirements. However, note that the macros and calorie values will change.

What is your most popular recipe?

Our smash burgers recipe has rapidly become our most popular recipe on the site, with its delicious combination of crispy, caramelized onions and juicy, quality meat.

How do I know if a recipe is gluten-free or dairy-free?

Unfortunately, none of our recipes are gluten-free or dairy-free. The recipes however, can be altered to better fit your dietery needs.

How long does it take to prepare and cook a recipe?

The time it takes to prepare and cook a recipe varies depending on the complexity and the recipe itself. Some recipes may take only a few minutes to prepare and cook, while others may take hours. The recipe should provide an estimated time scale for preparation and cooking, so make sure to check before you start.

Can I make substitutions for ingredients I dont have?

Yes, you can make substitutions for ingredients you don't have. However, keep in mind that substitutions may alter the flavor of the dish. It's best to choose a substitute that has a similar taste to the original ingredient.
